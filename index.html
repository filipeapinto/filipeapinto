Stuffistry is an Internet-of-Things (IoT) business platform provider. Stuffistry offers OEMs, service providers, sponsors and brokers a way to build consumer-centric solutions. Stuffistry represents a quantum leap forward because it offers consumers a way to own their connected-asset data. Stuffistry monetizes business transactions instead of data.
